Water Conservation and Management

Water has always been the most indispensable resource on our planet. But due to its abundance, it has come through harsh treatment and abuse over the last few centuries. Today we realise the importance of Clean Water and slowly taking steps towards conserving water and managing the water resources present around. In light of the World Water Day celebrated by the United Nations on 22nd March every year, Chemflux 2017 will celebrate and create awareness about the importance of Water Conservation and Management.

About Chemflux

Chemflux is the Annual National level Technical fest conducted by the students and the faculty of the Department of Chemical Engineering, SRM University. Right from its inception in the year of 2014, Chemflux has created a benchmark for technical symposiums, impacting over 1500 students with over 30+ events in its previous editions. A consortium of chemical engineers, the fest instigates the engineer within every participating student with a wide range of events and also provides them with opportunities to interact with experts from the industries.

This year’s team consists of Convener: Dr. K. Tamilarasan Co-Convener: Mrs. D.Nanditha Head of the Department: Dr. M.P. Rajesh Student Coordinators: Secretary: R. Saikiran Joint-Secretary: Kavya Sravanthi Treasurer: V. Abhilash
